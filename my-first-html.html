Steps to Make a Sandwich

  1. Grab bread, peanut butter, jelly and a knife.
  2. Dip knife into peanut butter.
  3. Spread peanut butter evenly onto one slice of bread.
  4. Repeat with jelly on another slice of bread.
  5. Combine both slices, forming the sandwich.
  6. Clean up and enjoy.
